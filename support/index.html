Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my Stripe payment link?

Go to Settings → Payments and paste your Stripe Payment Link URL. You can create a Payment Link in your Stripe Dashboard under Products → Payment Links. Once saved, it will be included automatically in every SMS you send.

Can I send payment links from the simulator?

No — SMS requires a real iPhone. The app will notify you if SMS is unavailable. Test the full workflow on a physical device before your first real job.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → TripleTap Pro → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchases on a new phone?

Go to Settings inside TripleTap Pro and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used when you originally subscribed.

Where is my data stored?

All your job data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. TripleTap Pro does not upload your job information to any server. Back up your iPhone regularly via iCloud to protect your data.

How do I add items to my Price Book?

Tap the Price Book tab, select a category (Labor, Parts, Travel, Other), then tap "Add Item" at the bottom. Enter the item name and price and tap Save.

What is Demo Mode?

Demo Mode (found in Settings) replaces your real Stripe payment link with a demo page in SMS messages. Use this when showing the app to other contractors or during testing so no real payment requests are sent.
